Ore Genesis and Critical Metal Enrichment in Gunga Pb-Zn Deposit

Published in: Journal of Geochemical Exploration (2025)

DOI: 10.1016/j.gexplo.2025.107771

🔍 Project Summary

This study uses deep learning and trace element geochemistry of sphalerite to classify the Gunga Pb-Zn deposit (Southern Pakistan) and understand its ore genesis and critical metal enrichment. The research integrates machine learning with S and Pb isotopic data.

🧠 Machine Learning Highlights

  • Deep Neural Network model with 94.7% accuracy
  • Classified into CD-type Pb-Zn deposit
  • Enriched in Ge, Cd, Ag, Sb

📊 Key Findings

  • Low-temperature ore formation (130–200°C)
  • High sulfur fugacity and reduced conditions
  • S sourced from both BSR and TSR processes
  • Pb sourced from upper continental crust
